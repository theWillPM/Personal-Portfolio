About Me:

Hello! My name is Willian and I'm super excited to be a part of the software development world! I've always liked programming since my first HTML class in 1998. I'm a very dedicated individual, straight-A student (Aiming for a 4.0 GPA), and I always go the extra mile. Well, if I only did what was asked of me, how would I be different from anyone else?

I've set aside my Civil Engineering career in Brazil to become a Software Developer in Canada. I figured it would be a great place to study programming. After all, Calgary is the birthplace of James Gosling, the inventor of Java.

I am fascinated by how much programming languages have evolved in the past two decades. How much easier it has become to dive into the programming world! I really admire those who dared to start in this field 10 or 20 years ago. Bravo!

I began studying programming one year before coming to Canada. From logics to C++, Java, Javascript, HTML and then some game designing courses. I drew all the gifs you see here myself and they'll be part of my game called "Project Chiara". I'll most likely rename it to Chiara Chronicles. It has a nicer ring to it, doesn't it?
